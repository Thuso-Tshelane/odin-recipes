Baked Chicken Curry Dip

Description:

This baked chicken curry dip, beautiful, interesting, and flavorful, is curry chicken in dip form. If there's a hungry crowd coming to watch the game, this easy-to-make dip will feed a whole bunch of people with just a little chicken. Serve with crackers, bread, or cut-up naan.

Ingredients:

Baked dip:

  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup plain greek yoghurt
  • 1/4 cup tomato paste
  • 1 lime juiced
  • 4 cloves garlic, finely minced or crushed
  • 2 tablespoons finely sliced cilantro stems
  • 1 tablespoons garam masala
  • 2 teaspoons smoked paprika
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons kosher salt, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon ground tumeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 3 cups roughly chopped cooked chicken
  • 1/2 cup thinly sliced green onions
  • 6 ounces grated morzzarella cheese, diveded

Garnish:

  • 1/4 cup minced jalapeno peppers
  • 1/4 cup minced red onion
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 450 degrees F(230 degrees C)
  2. Combine cream cheese, Greek yoghurt, tomato paste, lime juice, garlic cilantro stems, salt, pepper, graram masala, smoked paprika, cumin, salt, coriander, tumeric, ginger, black pepper, and cayenne ina bowl. Mix until combined with a spatula.
  3. Add green onions, 4 ounces morzzarella, and chicken, and use a spatula to fold everything together until combined
  4. Transfer into a 2-quart baking dish or deep pie pan, and spread out evenly. Top with remaining mozzarella cheese. Dust top with more cayenne if desired.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until curry dip is heated through and cheese on top is melted, about 20 minutes.
  6. Garnish top with minced jalapeno peppers, red onion, and chopped cilantro. Serve immediately.
